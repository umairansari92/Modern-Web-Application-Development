CHEMISTRY FORMULAS

Unorder List

  • Water Molecule: H2O

    • Two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom form a water molecule.

  • Carbon Dioxide: CO2

    • One carbon atom and two oxygen atoms form carbon dioxide.

  • Ammonia: NH3

    • One nitrogen atom and three hydrogen atoms form ammonia.

  • Sodium Chloride (Salt): N aCl

    • Sodium (Na) combines with chlorine (Cl) to form salt.

  • Sulfuric Acid: H2SO4

    • Two hydrogen atoms, one sulfur atom, and four oxygen atoms form sulfuric acid.
